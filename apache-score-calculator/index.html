ℹ️ About APACHE II

When to Use

  • ICU admission severity assessment
  • Outcome prediction and benchmarking
  • Research and quality improvement
  • Prognostication discussions with families

Limitations

  • Does not predict individual patient outcome
  • Scores may change during ICU stay
  • Less accurate in certain populations (trauma, burns)
  • Newer scores (APACHE III, IV, SOFA) may be more accurate
