📊 Holliday-Segar Method Reference

Body WeightHourly RateDaily Rate
First 10 kg4 mL/kg/hr100 mL/kg/day
Next 10 kg (10-20)2 mL/kg/hr50 mL/kg/day
Each kg above 201 mL/kg/hr20 mL/kg/day

Example: 25 kg child

  • First 10 kg: 10 × 4 = 40 mL/hr
  • Next 10 kg: 10 × 2 = 20 mL/hr
  • Remaining 5 kg: 5 × 1 = 5 mL/hr
  • Total: 65 mL/hr = 1,560 mL/day

💧 Fluid Deficit Signs

Mild (3-5%)

  • Dry mucous membranes
  • Slightly decreased urine
  • Normal vital signs
  • Cap refill <2 sec

Moderate (6-9%)

  • Tachycardia
  • Decreased skin turgor
  • Sunken eyes/fontanelle
  • Oliguria

Severe (10%+)

  • Hypotension
  • Altered mental status
  • Anuria
  • Shock - emergency!

Replace 1/2 in first 8h

  • Then 1/2 over next 16h
  • Add maintenance fluids
  • Monitor electrolytes
  • Recheck weight
