⚠️ Important Clinical Notes

Nasal cannula FiO2 varies with patient's breathing pattern (tidal volume, respiratory rate). Venturi masks provide more accurate FiO2 delivery. For COPD patients, maintain SpO2 88-92%. Always titrate oxygen to target SpO2 rather than arbitrary FiO2.
