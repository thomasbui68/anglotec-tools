Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the IELTS Band Score Calculator

How accurate is this IELTS calculator?
The calculator uses the official IELTS scoring algorithm. It rounds your average to the nearest 0.5 band, exactly as the British Council and IDP do. For example, an average of 6.75 rounds up to 7.0, while 6.25 rounds down to 6.0.
Do I need to create an account to use it?
No account, email, or signup is required. The calculator is 100% free and works instantly. Just visit the page, enter your scores, and get your result.
What's the difference between Academic and General Training scoring?
The Listening and Speaking sections use the same scoring for both tests. The Reading section is scored differently — Academic Reading is slightly harder, so you need more correct answers for the same band. The calculator lets you select which test type you're taking.
What IELTS score do I need for UK nursing registration?
The NMC (Nursing and Midwifery Council) requires an overall band of 7.0, with at least 7.0 in Listening, Reading, and Speaking, and 6.5 in Writing. Our calculator shows you exactly which sections need improvement to meet this requirement.
Can I use this calculator on my phone?
Yes! The calculator is fully responsive and works perfectly on mobile phones, tablets, and desktops. Study during your commute, on breaks, or anywhere.
How can I improve my IELTS score after using the calculator?
After calculating your band, check out our AI-powered IELTS Prep platform for personalized study plans, mock tests, speaking practice with AI feedback, and writing correction.
