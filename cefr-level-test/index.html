    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is the CEFR level test?

    The CEFR (Common European Framework of Reference for Languages) is the international standard for describing language ability. This test measures your English skills across 6 levels from A1 (beginner) to C2 (mastery).

    How accurate is this online English level test?

    This test provides a reliable estimate of your CEFR level based on grammar, vocabulary, and reading comprehension. For official certification, consider IELTS or Cambridge exams.

    How long does the test take?

    Most learners complete the 15 questions in 5–8 minutes. There is no timer, so take your time to answer accurately.

    Is this test really free?

    Yes, this CEFR level test is 100% free with no registration required. Get instant results and personalised recommendations at no cost.

    What CEFR level do I need for IELTS?

    Generally, B1 corresponds to IELTS 4–5, B2 to IELTS 5.5–6.5, C1 to IELTS 7–8, and C2 to IELTS 8.5–9. Most universities require B2 or higher.
