Frequently Asked Questions

Everything international healthcare professionals need to know about preparing for NHS careers.

What score do I need on OET for NMC registration?+
The NMC requires at least Grade C+ in Reading and Listening, and Grade B in Writing and Speaking. Our platform targets Grade B across all sections to give you the best chance of registration success.
How many OSCE scenarios do I need to practise?+
Our platform includes 805+ scenarios covering APIE, clinical skills, and professional values. Most successful candidates complete at least 100-150 scenarios before their exam, focusing especially on their weak areas identified by our AI.
Can I use this while working full-time?+
Absolutely! Our AI platform is available 24/7, so you can practise around your shifts. Many nurses practise during breaks, commutes, or after night shifts. Bite-sized modules let you make progress even with just 15 minutes a day.
How long does it take to prepare for the OSCE?+
Most candidates need 4-8 weeks of consistent preparation. Our AI creates a personalised plan based on your start date and exam date. International nurses typically need more time for APIE scenarios, while clinical skills may come more quickly.
Do you help with NHS job applications too?+
Yes! Our NHS Interview Preparation module covers common questions, NHS values, situational judgement tests, and communication scenarios. We also provide guidance on CV preparation and what NHS recruiters look for in international candidates.
