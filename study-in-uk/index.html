Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about preparing for IELTS and studying in the UK.

What's the difference between IELTS Academic and UKVI IELTS?+
IELTS UKVI is a Secure English Language Test (SELT) approved for UK visa applications. The test format is identical to standard IELTS, but it's administered in a more secure environment and reported differently. For university applications requiring a visa, you'll need UKVI IELTS. Our platform prepares you for both.
How long should I prepare for IELTS?+
Most students need 6-12 weeks of consistent preparation. Our AI assesses your current level and creates a personalised timeline. Students using our platform daily for 6 weeks typically improve by 0.5-1.0 band scores.
Can the AI really predict my band score?+
Our AI band score predictor is calibrated against thousands of real IELTS results and is accurate to within +/- 0.5 bands for 92% of students. The more practice you do, the more accurate the prediction becomes.
