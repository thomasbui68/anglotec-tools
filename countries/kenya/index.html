The Challenges Facing Kenyan Learners

Kenya's healthcare professionals are highly respected worldwide. Kenyan nurses bring dedication, clinical skill, and compassion to the NHS. But UK registration requires meeting specific English standards:

🏥

OET Grade B Requirement

The NMC requires Grade B in all OET sub-tests. Kenyan nurses often need targeted practice in writing (referral letters) and reading (medical document processing).

🎤

NHS Interview Preparation

Beyond OET, NHS job interviews assess communication skills, cultural awareness, and clinical reasoning. Many Kenyan candidates need practice with UK interview formats.

📝

Clinical Documentation Standards

NHS documentation follows specific protocols that differ from Kenyan practice. Learning UK letter formats, care plans, and handover styles is essential.

📋

OSCE Communication Assessment

The NMC OSCE tests patient communication, explanation of procedures, and professional interactions. Practice with NHS-style scenarios is critical for first-time pass rates.

⏰

Working at KNH & Beyond

Many Kenyan nurses work at Kenyatta National Hospital, Moi Teaching Hospital, or regional facilities while preparing. Flexible study that fits around shift work is essential.
