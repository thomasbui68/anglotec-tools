NMC Registration Requirements

NMC English Language Requirements

  • OET: Grade B (350+) in Listening, Reading, Writing AND Speaking
  • IELTS: 7.0 in Reading, Listening, Speaking; 6.5 in Writing
  • Combined: IELTS 7.0 (R,L,S) + 6.5 (W) accepted
  • IELTS scores below 6.5 in any band = rejected
  • OET Grade C or below in any skill = rejected
Important: NMC recently updated requirements (2023). You can now combine OET and IELTS scores from two tests taken within 6 months. All scores must meet minimum thresholds.

OET Score Ranges:

  • Grade A: 450-500 (IELTS 8.0-9.0)
  • Grade B: 350-440 (IELTS 7.0-7.5)
  • Grade C+: 300-340 (IELTS 6.5)
  • Grade C: 200-290 (IELTS 5.5-6.0)
  • Grade D: 100-190 (IELTS 4.0-5.0)
  • Grade E: 0-90 (Below IELTS 4.0)

OET vs IELTS for Nurses

  • OET uses healthcare-specific scenarios
  • IELTS is general academic English
  • Most nurses find OET Writing easier
  • OET Speaking uses nurse-patient roleplay
  • Both accepted by NMC, GMC, HCPC
