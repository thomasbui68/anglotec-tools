Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about learning languages with AI.

Can I really learn a language with an AI tutor?+
Yes! Studies show that consistent conversation practice is the most important factor in language acquisition. Our AI tutor Emma provides unlimited speaking practice, instant corrections, and personalised lessons - things that are impossible with traditional apps or even human tutors at scale. Many of our users reach conversational fluency in 3-6 months.
How accurate is the pronunciation feedback?+
Our pronunciation engine uses advanced speech recognition technology that analyses your phonemes, intonation, rhythm, and stress patterns. It identifies specific sounds you struggle with and provides targeted exercises to help you improve. It's like having a pronunciation coach available 24/7.
Can I switch between languages?+
Premium and Annual plans give you access to all 12 languages. You can switch between them anytime and your progress is saved separately for each language. Many of our users learn 2-3 languages simultaneously.
Are the certificates recognised?+
Our certificates are CEFR-aligned (Common European Framework of Reference) and indicate your proficiency level from A1 (beginner) to C2 (mastery). They're recognised by many employers and institutions as proof of language ability. For official immigration or university purposes, we recommend also taking official exams like DELE, JLPT, or HSK.
What's included in the 14-day free trial?+
The free trial gives you full access to all Premium features: all 12 languages, unlimited AI conversations, advanced pronunciation feedback, complete grammar courses, mock exams, and certificates. No credit card required. Upgrade only if you love the experience.
