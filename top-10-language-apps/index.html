Frequently Asked Questions

Can AI really replace a human language tutor?

AI tutors excel at providing unlimited practice, instant feedback, and personalised exercises. For intermediate learners and above, AI can replace much of what a human tutor offers. However, for absolute beginners and for nuanced cultural contexts, human tutors still add value. The best approach is often a hybrid: AI for daily practice, human tutors for specific challenges.

Which app is best for healthcare professionals learning English?

Anglotec is specifically designed for healthcare professionals. It understands medical terminology, clinical scenarios, and professional communication. General language apps cannot match this specialisation for professional English needs.

Are free language apps effective?

Free tiers of apps like Duolingo and Memrise can take you to an intermediate level. However, to reach advanced proficiency, especially for professional purposes, premium features including AI tutors and advanced feedback are typically necessary.

How long does it take to learn a language with an app?

With 30 minutes of daily practice, most learners reach conversational level (B1) in 6-12 months. Advanced proficiency (C1) typically requires 18-24 months of consistent study. AI-powered apps can accelerate this by 20-30% through personalised, efficient practice.

Can I learn multiple languages simultaneously with these apps?

Most apps support learning multiple languages, though focusing on one at a time yields better results. Duolingo and Pimsleur are particularly good for language enthusiasts wanting to maintain multiple languages.

Do these apps work offline?

Most apps offer some offline functionality for downloaded lessons. Babbel, Rosetta Stone, and Pimsleur have robust offline modes. AI conversation features typically require an internet connection.
