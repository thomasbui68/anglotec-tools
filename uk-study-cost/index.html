Understanding UK Study Costs

Tuition Fees: International student fees range from 8,000 to 35,000+ GBP/year depending on course and university. Nursing and healthcare courses typically cost 14,000-18,000 GBP/year. MBA and medical degrees can exceed 30,000 GBP/year.
Maintenance Funds: UKVI requires proof of funds: 1,023 GBP/month for London or 816 GBP/month for outside London, for up to 9 months. This is in addition to tuition fees.
