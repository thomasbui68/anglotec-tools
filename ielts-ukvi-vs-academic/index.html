When Do You Need IELTS UKVI?

Specific scenarios where IELTS UKVI is mandatory

🛂
Tier 4 Student Visa

UK Student Visa (Tier 4/Student Route)

If you're applying for a UK student visa and your course is below degree level, you MUST take IELTS UKVI. For degree-level courses, regular IELTS Academic may be accepted by your university, but check with your institution first.

🏥
Healthcare Visa

Health and Care Worker Visa

Nurses, doctors, and care workers applying for the Health and Care Worker visa need IELTS UKVI (or OET) to prove English proficiency. Minimum requirement: overall 7.0 with no band below 6.5 for nursing roles.

💼
Skilled Worker Visa

Skilled Worker Visa Applications

Professionals applying for the UK's Skilled Worker visa route need a SELT-approved test. IELTS UKVI is the most popular choice, requiring a minimum CEFR B1 level (IELTS 4.0 overall).

🏠
Settlement

Indefinite Leave to Remain (ILR)

Applicants for UK settlement or British citizenship through the Life in the UK route need IELTS UKVI at B1 level or above as proof of English language ability.

👨‍👩‍👧
Family Visa

Family Visa (Spouse/Partner)

Family visa applicants need to prove English at CEFR A1 or A2 level. IELTS UKVI Life Skills A1 or B1 is specifically designed for this route.

📋
NMC Registration

NMC & GMC Registration

While NMC accepts both IELTS Academic and UKVI for registration, some employers and visa categories specifically require the UKVI version. Always confirm with your recruiter.

When Is IELTS Academic Enough?

Scenarios where standard IELTS Academic is perfectly suitable

🎓

University Applications (Non-UK)

Applying to universities in Australia, Canada, New Zealand, Ireland, or the USA? Standard IELTS Academic is accepted everywhere and is the preferred choice for academic admissions.

📚

UK Degree-Level Courses (Direct Entry)

If your UK university has assessed your English level and confirmed they accept standard IELTS Academic for degree-level programmes, you may not need UKVI. Check your CAS statement.

🏛️

Professional Body Registration

Many professional bodies worldwide (engineering, accounting, law) accept standard IELTS Academic for credential evaluation and registration purposes.

💰

Budget-Conscious Test Takers

IELTS Academic costs approximately £37 less than UKVI. If you don't need the UKVI-specific reference number, Academic saves money without any disadvantage.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about IELTS UKVI vs Academic

Is the IELTS UKVI test harder than regular IELTS? +
No. The test content, format, difficulty, and scoring are identical. The only difference is administrative: UKVI tests are conducted at approved centres with additional security protocols, and your results include a unique UKVI reference number recognised by UK Visas & Immigration.
Can I use IELTS Academic for a UK student visa? +
Only if you're applying for a degree-level course AND your university has explicitly confirmed they sponsor students with standard IELTS Academic. For courses below degree level (pre-sessional, foundation, diploma), IELTS UKVI is mandatory. Always check your Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies (CAS).
Do UK universities prefer IELTS UKVI or Academic? +
Most UK universities accept both. The academic admissions team typically doesn't distinguish between them since the test content is identical. However, your university's visa sponsorship team may require UKVI. Contact both departments before booking.
How much more expensive is IELTS UKVI? +
IELTS UKVI costs approximately £222 in 2026, compared to £185 for standard IELTS Academic - a difference of about £37. IELTS Life Skills is typically around £150. Prices vary slightly by country and test centre.
How long are IELTS UKVI results valid? +
Both IELTS UKVI and Academic results are valid for 2 years from your test date. However, UKVI may require your test to have been taken within a specific timeframe for your visa application - typically within 2 years.
Can I switch from Academic to UKVI after booking? +
No, these are separate booking systems. If you book the wrong test, you'll need to cancel and rebook. Some test centres charge a cancellation fee. Always confirm which test you need with your university, employer, or immigration advisor before booking.
What IELTS score do I need for a UK nursing visa? +
The NMC requires IELTS 7.0 overall with at least 6.5 in each section (Reading, Writing, Listening, Speaking). For the Health and Care Worker visa, UKVI accepts this score. You can combine scores from two tests taken within 6 months of each other.

Top Tips for Choosing the Right Test

1

Check Your Visa Category First

Visit the official UK government website to see if your visa route requires a SELT test. If it does, IELTS UKVI is your safest option.

2

Confirm with Your University

Email both the admissions office AND the international student support team. Ask specifically: "Do you accept standard IELTS Academic for CAS issuance?"

3

Consider IELTS One Skill Retake

Both UKVI and Academic now offer One Skill Retake in many countries. If you narrowly miss your target in one section, you can retake just that section within 60 days.

4

Book Early at UKVI Centres

UKVI-approved test centres have limited availability and fill up faster. Book at least 4-6 weeks in advance, especially during peak seasons (July-September).

5

Use the Same Passport

Ensure your test booking name matches your passport exactly. UKVI is strict about name mismatches, and discrepancies can invalidate your results for visa purposes.
