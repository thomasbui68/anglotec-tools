Frequently Asked Questions

▶ What is PLAB 2 and who needs to take it?

PLAB 2 (Professional and Linguistic Assessments Board Part 2) is a practical OSCE exam that international medical graduates must pass to gain GMC registration and practise medicine in the UK. It tests your clinical skills through 16 simulated patient scenarios. You must pass PLAB 1 and have acceptable English (IELTS 7.5 or OET Grade B) before sitting PLAB 2.

▶ How long should I prepare for PLAB 2?

Most doctors need 8–16 weeks of dedicated preparation for PLAB 2. Our PLAB 2 online course is structured as a 12-week programme for the Complete package and 16 weeks for the Intensive package. If you have recent clinical experience and strong communication skills, you may need less time. Our diagnostic assessment at enrolment will recommend the ideal preparation duration for you.

▶ Can I really prepare for PLAB 2 online?

Absolutely. While PLAB 2 is a practical exam, our best PLAB 2 course combines live video mock OSCEs (where you perform scenarios with trainers via video), our AI-powered simulator (for unlimited practice), and detailed video demonstrations of every physical examination and procedure. Many of our students find online preparation MORE effective than in-person courses because they can practise more frequently and review recordings of their performance.

▶ How does the pass guarantee work?

Our PLAB 2 Intensive package includes a pass guarantee. If you complete all course requirements (attend 90% of live sessions, complete all AI simulations, pass 3 consecutive mock circuits with 12+/16) but still fail the actual PLAB 2 exam, we provide free additional coaching for your resit. ▶ What's included in the 1-to-1 mock OSCE sessions?

Each 1-to-1 mock OSCE session lasts 60–90 minutes and simulates a real PLAB 2 circuit. You'll complete 4–6 stations with a GMC-registered trainer playing the patient and/or examiner role. After each station, you receive detailed feedback on your clinical approach, communication skills, time management, and data interpretation. Sessions are recorded for your review. The Intensive package includes 20 of these sessions — more than any other PLAB 2 preparation course.

▶ Do you help with GMC registration after passing PLAB 2?

Yes! Our Complete and Intensive packages include a comprehensive GMC registration guide. We help you compile your evidence of fitness to practise, understand the registration requirements, and submit your application correctly. Many of our trainers have gone through the GMC registration process themselves and can share insider tips to avoid common delays.

▶ How often are your scenarios updated?

Our clinical scenario bank is updated monthly based on feedback from recent PLAB 2 candidates, changes in NICE guidelines, and new examination trends. Our medical education team reviews every scenario quarterly to ensure clinical accuracy and exam relevance. Intensive package students get access to all new scenarios as they're released.

▶ Can I study part-time while working?

Yes — many of our students are working doctors. Our PLAB 2 online course is designed for flexibility. All live sessions are recorded, the AI simulator is available 24/7, and 1-to-1 sessions can be scheduled around your shifts. We recommend 15–20 hours of study per week for 12 weeks, but you can spread this over a longer period if needed.
