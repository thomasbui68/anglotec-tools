Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Language Guide

Which languages are supported?
The Language Guide supports 12 languages: English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Arabic, Hindi, Filipino/Tagalog, Chinese (Mandarin), Japanese, and Korean. You can select any of these as your native language to learn medical English and daily conversation from.
Is this tool free?
Yes — the Language Guide is 100% free to use. No credit card, no subscription, and no signup required. All flashcards, exercises, grammar lessons, and AI conversation practice are available at no cost. Anglotec AI Academy is committed to making language learning accessible to all healthcare professionals.
Can I use this for medical English specifically?
Absolutely. The Language Guide includes extensive medical English vocabulary organized by over 50 clinical specialties including cardiology, respiratory, neurology, gastroenterology, orthopaedics, paediatrics, oncology, and many more. It's ideal for healthcare professionals preparing for OET, PLAB, or starting work in English-speaking healthcare environments.
How is this different from Duolingo or Babbel?
Unlike general language apps, the Language Guide is purpose-built for healthcare professionals. It focuses on medical terminology, hospital daily conversation, UK healthcare cultural context, and professional communication — none of which Duolingo or Babbel cover. Our AI tutor also understands medical scenarios and provides context-aware feedback relevant to clinical settings.
Does it work offline?
The Language Guide requires an internet connection as it uses AI-powered features for real-time conversation practice, pronunciation feedback, and interactive exercises. However, your progress and bookmarks are saved and sync across all your devices automatically.
Can healthcare professionals get a certificate?
While the Language Guide itself does not issue certificates, it is an excellent preparation tool for recognised exams like OET (Occupational English Test) and IELTS, which are accepted by the NMC, GMC, and other UK healthcare regulators. We recommend using the Language Guide alongside our IELTS Calculator and OET prep resources to track your exam readiness.
