▶ What's the difference between Academic and General Training IELTS?

Our online IELTS preparation covers both Academic and General Training modules. Academic IELTS is required for university admissions and professional registration (e.g., GMC, NMC). General Training is for immigration and work purposes. The Listening and Speaking sections are identical; only Reading and Writing differ. When you enrol, simply tell us which module you need, and we'll tailor your study plan accordingly.
