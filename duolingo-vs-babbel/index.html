Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use Duolingo to prepare for IELTS? +
No. Duolingo teaches basic vocabulary and grammar through gamified exercises, but it doesn't cover IELTS test format, academic writing, timed reading strategies, or speaking test simulation. The Duolingo English Test (DET) is a separate product and accepted by far fewer institutions than IELTS. For IELTS preparation, you need a dedicated exam prep tool like Anglotec AI.
Is Babbel better than Duolingo for serious learners? +
Yes, for learners who want structured grammar instruction and natural dialogues. Babbel's courses are designed by linguists and follow a clear progression. However, both apps are designed for general language learning, not exam preparation. If you need to pass IELTS, OET, or PTE, neither Duolingo nor Babbel will be sufficient on their own.
What makes Anglotec AI different from other apps? +
Anglotec AI is purpose-built for English exam preparation (IELTS, OET, PTE) and professional English. Unlike general language apps, it uses advanced AI to provide band-specific feedback on writing, simulate speaking tests with scoring, and create personalised study plans based on your target score and deadline. The medical English content for OET is unique in the market.
Can I use these apps together? +
Absolutely. Many successful learners combine Duolingo for daily vocabulary building, Babbel for grammar foundations, and Anglotec AI for exam-specific preparation. However, if you have a tight exam deadline, prioritise Anglotec AI as it focuses directly on the skills and strategies that improve your test score.
Is the Duolingo English Test accepted instead of IELTS? +
The DET is accepted by approximately 4,500 institutions compared to 11,000+ for IELTS. Many top universities, the UK NMC, GMC, and Australian AHPRA do NOT accept the DET. Always check with your specific institution or regulatory body before taking the DET. IELTS remains the gold standard for academic and professional English certification.
Which is the best free option? +
For casual learning: Duolingo's free tier is unbeatable. For exam preparation: Anglotec AI offers a generous free tier with limited daily practice sessions, AI feedback, and one mock test. Babbel requires a paid subscription for meaningful use. For exam prep on a budget, start with Anglotec's free plan.
