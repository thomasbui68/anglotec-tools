OET Speaking – Nursing Role-Play Preparation: 3 min | Speaking: 5 min

Patient Discharge Planning

Setting: Hospital ward
Task: Speak to Mrs. Anderson (played by AI examiner), a 67-year-old patient being discharged after hip replacement surgery. Explain the discharge plan, discuss home care arrangements, address her concerns about managing at home, and provide follow-up appointment details. Use an empathetic and professional tone.

Skills Tested: Empathetic communication, explaining procedures, addressing concerns, professional nursing language
OET Writing – Referral Letter Time: 45 minutes | Words: 180-200

Referral to Community Nurse

Task: Using the case notes provided, write a letter to the community nurse, Ms. Sarah Jenkins, requesting a home visit assessment for Mr. Robert Chen (78), recently discharged after a stroke. Include his current mobility status, medication regime, care needs, and specific requests for the community nurse assessment.

Skills Tested: Professional letter format, information selection, appropriate language, organisation and coherence
OET Speaking – Difficult Conversation Preparation: 3 min | Speaking: 5 min

Medication Non-Compliance

Setting: GP Practice
Task: Mr. David Thompson, 45, has been skipping his diabetes medication. His latest HbA1c is 9.2%. Speak to him about the importance of medication compliance, address his concerns about side effects he read about online, and explain the risks of uncontrolled diabetes. Maintain a non-judgmental approach.

Skills Tested: Difficult conversations, health education, addressing misinformation, motivational interviewing
