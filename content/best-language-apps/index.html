★★★★☆ 4.6/5

Duolingo remains the most downloaded language app for good reason. Its gamified approach makes daily practice genuinely addictive, and the free tier provides substantial value. With 40+ languages, it covers options no competitor offers (Klingon, High Valyrian, Welsh, etc.).
