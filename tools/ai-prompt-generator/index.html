Anatomy of a Great Prompt

Master these 6 elements to craft prompts that deliver extraordinary results every time

🎯

Clear Role

Assign a specific role to the AI (e.g., "Act as a senior Python developer") to frame its expertise and perspective.

💡

Context

Provide relevant background information so the AI understands the full picture and constraints.

📝

Specific Task

Define exactly what you want the AI to produce. Vague tasks produce vague results.

📏

Output Format

Specify how you want the response structured — bullet points, tables, code, JSON, etc.

🔭

Constraints

Set boundaries like word count, tone, exclusions, or specific requirements to refine output.

🔎

Chain of Thought

For complex tasks, ask the AI to think step-by-step before giving its final answer.
