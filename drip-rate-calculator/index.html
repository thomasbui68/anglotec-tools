Common Drip Sets

  • Microdrip (60 gtt/mL): Pediatric, precise medication dosing
  • Macrodrip (20 gtt/mL): Standard adult IV, blood transfusions
  • Macrodrip (15 gtt/mL): Common general infusion set
  • Macrodrip (10 gtt/mL): Rapid fluid replacement

Key Formulas

  • Drops/min: (Volume × Drop Factor) / Time (min)
  • Infusion Time: Volume / Flow Rate
  • Flow Rate (mL/hr): Volume / Time (hours)
