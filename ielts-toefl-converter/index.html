Key Differences

  • IELTS: Band 0-9, pen-paper or computer, British/Australian accent, face-to-face speaking
  • TOEFL: Score 0-120, internet-based (iBT), American accent, recorded speaking
  • Validity: Both scores valid for 2 years from test date
