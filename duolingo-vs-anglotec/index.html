FAQs

Can Duolingo replace IELTS preparation?

No. Duolingo does not teach IELTS exam strategies, academic writing, or speaking test formats. For IELTS, you need a dedicated prep platform like Anglotec.

Is the Duolingo English Test accepted for nursing registration?

No. NMC UK, AHPRA Australia, and other major nursing regulators do not accept the Duolingo English Test. They require IELTS or OET.

Should I use both apps?

Yes - many students use Duolingo for daily vocabulary building and Anglotec for focused exam preparation. They complement each other well.
